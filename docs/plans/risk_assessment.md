# Risk Assessment

## High Priority Risks

### 1. Technical Debt

- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Description**: Accumulation of technical debt from rapid development may impact long-term maintainability
- **Mitigation**:
  - Regular code reviews
  - Dedicated refactoring sprints
  - Quality gates in CI/CD
  - Technical debt tracking
- **Owner**: Lead Developers
- **Status**: Active monitoring

### 2. Resource Shortage

- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Low
- **Description**: Insufficient team members or skills to meet project demands
- **Mitigation**:
  - Cross-training team members
  - Flexible resource allocation
  - Early hiring planning
  - Knowledge documentation
- **Owner**: Project Manager
- **Status**: Low risk

## Medium Priority Risks

### 1. Timeline Delays

- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Description**: Project milestones may be delayed due to unforeseen issues
- **Mitigation**:
  - Buffer time in schedule
  - Regular progress monitoring
  - Early risk identification
  - Agile methodology for flexibility
- **Owner**: Project Manager
- **Status**: Active monitoring

### 2. Integration Challenges

- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Description**: Difficulties integrating various system components
- **Mitigation**:
  - Early integration testing
  - Clear interface definitions
  - Regular team communication
  - Incremental integration approach
- **Owner**: Technical Leads
- **Status**: Active monitoring

### 3. Performance Issues

- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Description**: System may not meet performance requirements
- **Mitigation**:
  - Early performance testing
  - Performance optimization sprints
  - Load testing
  - Monitoring and alerting
- **Owner**: DevOps Engineer
- **Status**: Active monitoring

## Low Priority Risks

### 1. Third-Party Dependencies

- **Impact**: Low
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Description**: Issues with third-party services or libraries
- **Mitigation**:
  - Dependency monitoring
  - Alternative solutions identified
  - Regular updates
  - Vendor communication
- **Owner**: DevOps Engineer
- **Status**: Low risk

### 2. Scope Creep

- **Impact**: Low
- **Probability**: Low
- **Description**: Uncontrolled expansion of project scope
- **Mitigation**:
  - Clear requirements documentation
  - Change control process
  - Regular stakeholder communication
  - Prioritization framework
- **Owner**: Project Manager
- **Status**: Low risk

## Risk Response Plans

### Immediate Response (Within 24 hours)

- Risk assessment team meeting
- Impact analysis
- Initial mitigation actions
- Stakeholder notification (if needed)

### Short-term Response (Within 1 week)

- Detailed mitigation plan
- Resource allocation
- Timeline adjustments
- Progress monitoring

### Long-term Response (Ongoing)

- Continuous monitoring
- Regular updates
- Lessons learned documentation
- Process improvements

## Risk Communication

### Internal Communication

- Daily standups: Active risks

### External Communication

- Management: Risk summary and trends
